DARKSEID

About Darkseid

Darkseid is a fictional supervillain appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by writer-artist Jack Kirby and first appears in Superman's Pal Jimmy Olsen #134 .

Author's Perspective

In my opinion, I believe Darkseid is the worst villain of them all. I think he could defeat all of DC's superheros and conquer the universe. After the destruction of his home planet Apokolips, he became a fearsome foe, vowing to cause pain to everyone he came across.
